Training with IoT

The Devices

Differente Sensors were used such as wind speed and direction, Inertial Movement Unit (IMU), GPS, Battery Charge, Relays for Warning Lights, a horn for sounds, a Water Depth and Temperature Sensor and a Water Flow Sensor and RGB Led Matrix connected trough Internet achieved by 4G MiFi Router and WiFi.

Interconnections

Devices are digital and interconnected through Internet data traveling by WiFi, 4G, MQTT.

The Project

The data acquired by IoT Devices are recorded in only one system integrated with the Server App. Devices have its own local Web-App as well.
